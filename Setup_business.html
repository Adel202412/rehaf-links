1. Mainland Licences in Abu Dhabi

1.1 Tajer Abu Dhabi Licence (Trader Licence)

Best for: Freelancers, small businesses, and startups that want to avoid office rental costs.
Eligibility: UAE nationals, GCC nationals, and foreign residents.
Office Requirement: Not required for the first 3 years.

Steps:

  1. Apply via TAMM using Emirates ID.
  2. Select from 1,200+ business activities.
  3. Pay fees and receive e-licence.

Costs (official):
Licence issuance: AED 790 (includes 6 activities).
Extra activity: AED 100 each.
Chamber membership: AED 50 (+ AED 100 after 6 activities).

1.2 Standard Economic Licence (Mainland)

Best for: Companies requiring a physical office and broader commercial activity.

Steps:

  1. Reserve trade name (TAMM).
  2. Submit initial approval.
  3. Prepare MoA/AoA, tenancy (Tawtheeq), Emirates IDs.
  4. Apply via ADRA.
  5. Pay fees ‚Üí issue licence.
  6. Register with Abu Dhabi Chamber.

Costs (official):
Licence issuance: AED 1,200 (6 activities included).
Additional activity: AED 100.
Chamber membership: AED 50 (+ AED 100 after 6 activities).
